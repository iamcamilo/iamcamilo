Universítame

brand identity ui & ux design front-end development

Universítame was an online community where relationships are created among pre-university students and students who are immersed in college. They could share accurate information, generating a personalized counseling.

I was part of the founder team when this startup was selected as part of the 11th generation of StartupChile.

I had the responsibility of designing the brand, Landing page and the UI & UX of the app for desktop and mobile. Also, I had the luck of working on the Front-end side of the app too, translating all the components and views into HTML, CSS and Js.
